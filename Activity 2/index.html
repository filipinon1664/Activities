Asado Pork Siopao

A quick and tasty meal for anyone can cook.

Ingredients

  • Flour: Typically all-purpose flour, sometimes with rice flour or cake flour.
  • Active dry yeast for the dough to rise.
  • Sweetener: Sugar (granulated white or brown).
  • Liquid: Lukewarm water or milk, often with a bit of oil or shortening for softness.
  • Protein: Ground pork is traditional for siopao asado, while siopao bola-bola may use a mix of pork, ground shrimp, and salted egg.

    • steps

    1. Set up your steamer: Arrange the siopao on a steamer tray, ensuring they arent touching. You can place them on squares of parchment paper to prevent sticking.
    2. Bring water to a boil: Bring the water in the steamer to a boil and place the siopao inside.
    3. Steam: Cover the steamer and steam the siopao for about 15 to 25 minutes. Adjust the time based on your equipment and whether the buns are frozen or fresh.
    4. Prevent soggy buns: Cover the steamer lid with a cloth to catch condensation and prevent it from dripping onto the buns, which can make them soggy.
